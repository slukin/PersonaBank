A narrator long ago went back to a cafeteria, saw a woman with an acquaintance and at a table and he began to be in love with the woman.  

The narrator walked toward the woman and the acquaintance.  

The narrator greeted the woman and the acquaintance.  

The narrator sat down at the table.  

The narrator offered the woman an every thing of the cafeteria.  

The woman thought that the narrator was insane.  

The narrator departed the woman and the acquaintance.
